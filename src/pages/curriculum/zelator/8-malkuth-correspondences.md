The 1 = 10 grade of Zelator is attributed to the sphere of Malkuth on the Qabalistic Tree of Life.This is the first grade that actually places the aspiring magician on the Tree of Life. Malkuth has the additional titles of the Gate, and the Inferior Mother, and it is the ultimate Sephirah of form and manifestation.

- Name in English: The Kingdom
- Divine (Atziluthic) Name: Adonai Ha-Aretz
- Archangel (Briatic) Name: Sandalphon
- Angelic (Yetziratic) Choir: Ashim
- Planet or House of Assiah: Olam ha-Yesodoth
- Spiritual experience: Vision of the Holy Guardian Angel
- Qabalistic Number: Ten. 55 (the sum of 1-10; and the number of material times the number of magick). 496 is the enumeration of MLKVTh, which reduces to 19 -> 10.
- Color (Briatic): Citrine, russet, olive, black
- Element: Earth
- Part of Anatomy: Feet, Anus
- Tarot cards: The Four Tens, The Four Princesses
- Magical Image: A young woman, crowned and throned
- Magical Formula: VITRIOL
- Animals: the Bull & Cow; the Sphinx
- Plants: Corn, Wheat, Ivy, Rice
- Incense: Dittany of Crete, Patchouli (commonly used in wealth magick), Pine, Vetivert
- Gemstone: Rock crystal, salt
- Symbols: Double cube altar, equal-armed cross, the magick circle, the triangle of art (evocation)
- Lineal Figure: Dekagram
- Virtue: Discrimination
- Vice: Inertia, Avarice
- Gods: Egyptian, Geb; Sumerian, Ninhursaga or Aruru; Greek, Gaea and Demeter; Roman, Tellus and Ceres.

It is your job to memorize all of this information pertaining to the element of Earth and the Sephirah of Malkuth. Practice your visualization by building a memory palace for this information.
