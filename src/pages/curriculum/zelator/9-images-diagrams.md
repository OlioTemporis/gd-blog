A description of different GD symbols like:

- The Laver of Water of Purification = the Waters of Binah, the female power reflected in the Waters of Creation
- The Flyfot Cross = the Admission Badge to Zelator, also called the Hermetic Cross, the Hammer of Thor, and the Swastika. It is made out of seventeen squares (Sol + four elements + twelve Zodiac signs)
- The Table of Shewbread = the twelve simple letters or signs of the Zodiac
- The Seven-Branched Candlestick = the seven double letters or planets
